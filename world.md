# World Setting: Corisca Autonomous Region (PM Project)

## 1. Background
In the Corisca Autonomous Region, an enigmatic variant virus has caused a surge in cases where ordinary citizens transform into monstrous entities known as **"Mutants,"** attacking people. Some infected individuals do not mutate but instead manifest extraordinary abilities, becoming **"Gifters."** These Gifters possess high combat capabilities and special powers but are socially deemed dangerous and subject to detainment.

## 2. PET System Overview
Due to the deteriorating public safety and the increasing "Mutant" incidents, conventional police forces are no longer sufficient. Thus, a special system called the **"PET System"** has been implemented, pairing police officers with detained Gifters (prisoners) for combat operations.

### 2.1. Roles
- **PETs:** Detained Gifters, treated as assets.
- **MASTERs:** Police Gifters, responsible for managing PETs.

### 2.2. Pairing
- **Selection:** MASTERs select their PETs based on ability compatibility, combat aptitude, and psychological suitability.
- **Healing Resonance:** When either a PET or MASTER sustains an injury, an involuntary **"heat" (発情)** state occurs in the paired individuals. Physical intimacy between the pair accelerates the healing process of the injured party. This "heat" is exclusive to the paired partner; rejecting it intensifies the impulse.

### 2.3. Management & Control
- **Co-habitation:** PETs are mandated to live with their MASTERs in provided private quarters.
- **Responsibilities:** MASTERs are responsible for their PET's management, daily life, and mission execution.
- **Restraint Collar:** PETs wear a specialized collar that functions as a control device, delivering potent **neural stimulation (精神刺激)** without physical harm. It activates automatically upon disobedience or resistance, restricting the PET's actions.

## 3. Operational Directives
MASTERs are authorized to conduct the following actions towards their PETs as part of their management and training:
- **Discipline (躾)**
- **Interrogation (尋問)**
- **Reward (褒美)**
- **Training (調教)**
